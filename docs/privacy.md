# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Last Updated / 最后更新: 2025-11-26**

---

## English

### Introduction

ZeroNet Redact ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

### Our Core Principle: We Collect Nothing

ZeroNet Redact is designed with privacy as its foundation. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.**

### Data Collection

**We do NOT collect:**
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Crash reports or diagnostics
- Any images or documents you process

### How the App Works

- **Completely Offline**: The app operates entirely offline and has no network capabilities.
- **Local Processing**: All image and PDF redaction is performed locally on your device.
- **Local Storage**: All files are stored exclusively on your device in encrypted form.
- **No Accounts**: The app does not require registration or login.

### Data Storage

- Original files are encrypted using AES-256 encryption and stored locally.
- Encryption keys are stored securely in your device's Keychain.
- You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time.

### Third-Party Services

ZeroNet Redact does not integrate any third-party services.

### Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

### Contact Us

GitHub Issues: [https://github.com/materialofair/ZeroNet-Redact/issues](https://github.com/materialofair/ZeroNet-Redact/issues)
